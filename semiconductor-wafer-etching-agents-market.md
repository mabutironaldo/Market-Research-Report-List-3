<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Etching Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Etching Agent</li><li>Dry Etching Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor wafer etching agents are used to remove material from semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process. Wet etching agents involve immersing the wafer in a liquid solution to dissolve unwanted material, while dry etching agents use plasma or gas to achieve the same result. Both types of etching agents are important in semiconductor manufacturing, with wet etching agents being more commonly used due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while dry etching agents are favored for their precision and ability to etch smaller features.</p></p>
