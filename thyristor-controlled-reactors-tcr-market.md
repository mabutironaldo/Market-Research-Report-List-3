<p><p>A Thyristor Controlled Reactor (TCR) is a type of reactive power compensation device used in high voltage power systems to regulate the flow of reactive power. It works by controlling the impedance of the reactor using thyristors, which are semiconductor devices. TCRs are mainly used in applications such as voltage regulation, power factor correction, and dynamic reactive power compensation in electric grids.</p><p>The Thyristor Controlled Reactors (TCR) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
