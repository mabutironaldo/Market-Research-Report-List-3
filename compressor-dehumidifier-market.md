<p><p>Compressor dehumidifiers come in two main types: tower type and rotor type. Tower type dehumidifiers are tall and slim, making them ideal for spaces with limited floor space. They work by pulling in air through the bottom and releasing dry air at the top. On the other hand, rotor type dehumidifiers use a rotating wheel to absorb moisture from the air, making them more efficient in drying large areas quickly. Both types are effective in reducing humidity levels and improving air quality in indoor spaces.</p></p>
