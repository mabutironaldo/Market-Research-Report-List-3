<p><ul><li>DC Magnetic Flowmeter</li><li>Induction Magnetic Flowmeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Line Magnetic Flowmeters are of two types: DC Magnetic Flowmeters and Induction Magnetic Flowmeters. DC Magnetic Flowmeters use a direct current to generate a magnetic field and measure the voltage produced as a conductive liquid flows through the pipe, while Induction Magnetic Flowmeters induce a magnetic field into the liquid and measure the voltage generated. Both types of flowmeters are widely used in various industries to accurately measure the flow rate of conductive liquids.</p></p>
