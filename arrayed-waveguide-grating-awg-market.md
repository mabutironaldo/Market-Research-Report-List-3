<p><ul><li>Thermal AWG</li><li>Athermal AWG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arrayed Waveguide Gratings (AWGs) are commonly divided into two types: Thermal AWG and Athermal AWG. Thermal AWGs require precise temperature control to maintain stable signal transmission, making them more suitable for controlled environments. On the other hand, Athermal AWGs are designed to operate effectively without the need for temperature control, offering greater flexibility and reliability in various operating conditions. Both types of AWGs play crucial roles in wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) systems for telecommunications and data transmission applications.</p></p>
