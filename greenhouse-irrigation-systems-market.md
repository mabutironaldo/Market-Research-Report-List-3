<p><p>Greenhouse Irrigation Systems market includes various types such as Drip Irrigation, Micro Sprinkler Irrigation, Sprinkler Irrigation, and Others. Drip Irrigation involves delivering water directly to the base of plants, reducing water waste. Micro Sprinkler Irrigation uses small sprinklers to evenly distribute water. Sprinkler Irrigation involves spraying water over crops from above. Other systems may include fogging systems or capillary mats. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of greenhouse growers, ensuring efficient and effective water distribution for plant growth.</p></p>
