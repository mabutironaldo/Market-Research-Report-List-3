<p><p>Semiconductor laser diode chips are used in various markets, with two main types being Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) and Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Lasers (VCSEL). EELs emit light from the edge of the chip, making them suitable for long-distance communication applications. VCSELs emit light perpendicular to the chip surface, making them ideal for short-distance applications, such as data communication within data centers. Both types of laser diode chips have their own unique advantages and are widely used in different market sectors.</p></p>
