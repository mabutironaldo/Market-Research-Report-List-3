<p><ul><li>Flue Cured Organic Tobacco</li><li>Air Cured Organic Tobacco</li><li>Sun Cured Organic Tobacco</li><li>Fire Cured Organic Tobacco</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic tobacco market can be classified into four types based on the curing process: Flue Cured Organic Tobacco, Air Cured Organic Tobacco, Sun Cured Organic Tobacco, and Fire Cured Organic Tobacco. Flue cured tobacco is dried using indirect heat, air cured tobacco is dried by hanging in a well-ventilated barn, sun cured tobacco is dried outdoors under the sun, and fire cured tobacco is dried using direct smoke. Each type of curing process imparts unique flavors and characteristics to the tobacco, catering to different preferences in the market.</p></p>
