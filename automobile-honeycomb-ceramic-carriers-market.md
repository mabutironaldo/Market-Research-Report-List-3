<p><strong>The Automobile Honeycomb Ceramic Carriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight through carrier</li><li>Wall Flow Carrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile honeycomb ceramic carriers come in two main types: straight through carriers and wall flow carriers. Straight through carriers allow the exhaust gases to flow straight through the channels, whereas wall flow carriers force the gases to pass through the porous walls, providing higher filtration efficiency. Straight through carriers are more commonly used in gasoline engines, while wall flow carriers are preferred for diesel engines due to their ability to capture more particulate matter. Both types play a crucial role in reducing emissions and improving air quality.</p></p>
