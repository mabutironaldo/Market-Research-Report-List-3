<p><strong>The Cyber-Physical Production System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EP-CPS</li><li>IT-CPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cyber-Physical Production System (CPPS) market can be broadly categorized into two types: EP-CPS and IT-CPS. EP-CPS refers to the Embedded Product Cyber-Physical Production System, which focuses on integrating cyber-physical technology directly into physical products. This allows for real-time communication and coordination among interconnected products and machines. On the other hand, IT-CPS stands for Information Technology Cyber-Physical Production System, which involves the application of information technology to enhance the efficiency and reliability of production processes, including data analytics, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. Both types aim to improve productivity and optimize the manufacturing process.</p></p>
