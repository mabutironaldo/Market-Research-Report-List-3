<p><strong>The Electronic Medical Records Software Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based electronic medical records (EMR) software solutions involve storing patient data on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-premise EMR solutions, on the other hand, involve hosting patient data locally on a healthcare facility's servers, providing more control and security but requiring higher upfront costs. Both types cater to different organizational needs, with cloud-based solutions being popular among smaller practices and on-premise solutions preferred by larger institutions needing more customization and security.</p></p>
