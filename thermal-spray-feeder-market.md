<p><p>Thermal spray feeders come in two main types: wire form and powder form. Wire form feeders use a continuous wire feedstock that is melted and sprayed onto a surface to create a protective coating. Powder form feeders, on the other hand, use powdered materials that are fed into a spray gun and melted before being sprayed onto a surface. Both types of feeders are commonly used in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for applications such as corrosion protection and surface restoration.</p></p>
