<p><p>Organic skin toner market consists of two main types: Fluid and Mist. Fluid toners are liquid-based and are typically applied to the skin using a cotton pad, while Mist toners are sprayed onto the skin in a fine mist form. Fluid toners are often favored for their deep cleansing and pore-refining properties, while Mist toners are popular for their ease of application and refreshing feel. Both types of toners help to balance the skin's pH levels and prep the skin for better absorption of subsequent skincare products.</p></p>
