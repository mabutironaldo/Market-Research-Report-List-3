<p><p>Functional flours are specialized ingredients that serve specific purposes beyond basic nutrition. Two main types of functional flours are Cereal and Legume flours. Cereal flours, like wheat or rice flour, are derived from grains and are commonly used in baking and cooking. Legume flours, like chickpea or lentil flour, come from dried beans and are known for their high protein content. Both types of functional flours offer unique nutritional benefits and can be used in a variety of recipes to enhance flavor and texture.</p></p>
