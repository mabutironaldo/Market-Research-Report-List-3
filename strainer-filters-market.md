<p><p>Strainer filters market includes various types such as Standard Cast Pipeline Strainers, Automatic Backwashing Strainers, Mechanically Cleaned Strainers, and others. Standard Cast Pipeline Strainers are widely used for removing solid particles from pipelines. Automatic Backwashing Strainers are designed to remove debris continuously without manual intervention. Mechanically Cleaned Strainers use a rotating filter element to remove particles from the fluid. Other types of strainer filters may include magnetic filters, basket strainers, and duplex strainers, each with unique features and applications in different industries.</p></p>
