<p><p>Medium pressure hoses are commonly classified into two main types based on their reinforcement materials: fabric reinforced and steel wire reinforced. Fabric reinforced hoses are made with layers of high-quality fabric materials that provide flexibility, durability, and resistance to abrasion. On the other hand, steel wire reinforced hoses utilize steel wire braids or spirals to enhance strength and withstand higher pressures. Both types of hoses are widely used in various industrial applications that require medium pressure handling capabilities.</p></p>
