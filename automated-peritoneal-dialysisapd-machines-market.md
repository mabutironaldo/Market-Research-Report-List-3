<p><p>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines are used to treat kidney failure by removing waste and excess fluid from the body. There are two main types of APD machines in the market: Power Type and Gravity Type. Power Type machines use an electric pump to cycle the dialysis solution in and out of the peritoneal cavity, offering more flexibility and faster dialysis sessions. Gravity Type machines rely on the force of gravity to move the dialysis solution, requiring fewer components and being more cost-effective. Both types have their advantages and are used based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
