<p><p>The Automatic Self-Cleaning Strainer market consists of two main types: Mechanically Cleaned and Backwashing strainers. Mechanically Cleaned strainers use a rotating mechanism to remove debris from the filtration screen, while Backwashing strainers use a reverse flow of water to clean the screen. Both types are designed to automate the cleaning process, reducing maintenance and ensuring continuous operation. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as the type of debris expected and the required level of automation.</p></p>
