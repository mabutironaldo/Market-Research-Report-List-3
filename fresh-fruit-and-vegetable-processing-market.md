<p><p>Fresh fruit and vegetable processing markets can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic processing involves the use of machinery and technology to carry out various stages of processing, such as washing, sorting, peeling, cutting, and packaging, with minimal human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic processing involves a combination of manual labor and machinery, with some tasks being performed by machines while others require human input. Both types of markets aim to increase efficiency, reduce labor costs, and ensure consistent quality in processed fruits and vegetables.</p></p>
