<p><p>Mechanical pencils come in two main types: screw-based and ratchet-based. Screw-based mechanical pencils operate by twisting the top of the pencil to extend or retract the lead, providing precision control. On the other hand, ratchet-based mechanical pencils feature a clicking mechanism that advances the lead with each click, making them convenient for quick writing or sketching. Both types offer advantages depending on the user's preferences for ease of use and control.</p></p>
