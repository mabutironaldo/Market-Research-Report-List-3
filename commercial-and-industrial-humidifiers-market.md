<p><p>Commercial and Industrial Humidifiers come in various types, including Adiabatic, Direct Injection Steam, and Heated Tank market. Adiabatic humidifiers use the principle of evaporative cooling to add moisture to the air, while Direct Injection Steam humidifiers inject steam directly into the air stream. Heated Tank humidifiers heat water to create steam that is then released into the air. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications, depending on factors such as required humidity levels, space constraints, and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
