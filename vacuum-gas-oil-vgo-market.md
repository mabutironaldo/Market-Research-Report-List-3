<p><ul><li>Heavy Vacuum Gas Oil (HVGO)</li><li>Light Vacuum Gas Oil (LVGO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum Gas Oil (VGO) is divided into two main types: Heavy Vacuum Gas Oil (HVGO) and Light Vacuum Gas Oil (LVGO). HVGO is a heavier, more viscous product with a higher boiling point, making it suitable for producing lubricating oils and asphalt. On the other hand, LVGO is a lighter product used for gasoline blending and as a feedstock for fluid catalytic cracking units. The market for VGO is influenced by the demand for both HVGO and LVGO, with prices fluctuating accordingly.</p></p>
