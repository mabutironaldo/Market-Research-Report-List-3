<p><p>Cosmetic additives market can be categorized into different types based on their functions. Solvents are used to dissolve other ingredients and improve the formulation of cosmetic products. Preservatives are added to prolong the shelf-life of products and prevent microbial growth. Emulsifiers help in stabilizing the mixture of oil and water-based ingredients in cosmetics. Antioxidants are added to prevent oxidation and maintain the quality of products. Other additives can include thickeners, colorants, fragrances, and UV filters, among others.</p></p>
