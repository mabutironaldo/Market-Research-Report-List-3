<p><p>PC Films market can be categorized into different types based on their properties and applications. Optical PC Films are used for display screens and optical components due to their high transparency and clarity. Flame Retardant PC Films are designed to prevent fires and are commonly used in electronics and construction materials. Weatherable PC Films are resistant to UV radiation and harsh weather conditions, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Other types of PC Films include conductive films, anti-glare films, and printable films, each serving specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
