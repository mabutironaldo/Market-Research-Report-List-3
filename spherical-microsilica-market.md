<p><ul><li>Sol Gel Technology</li><li>Ion Flame Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spherical microsilica is produced using two main technologies: sol-gel technology and ion flame technology. Sol-gel technology involves the creation of the microsilica particles through a chemical process using a liquid precursor. On the other hand, ion flame technology involves vaporizing silicon tetrachloride in a high-temperature plasma, resulting in the formation of spherical microsilica particles. Both technologies result in high-quality, spherical microsilica with unique properties that make them ideal for use in various industries such as construction, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
