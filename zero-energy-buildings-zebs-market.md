<p><p>Zero Energy Buildings (ZEBs) are buildings that are designed to generate as much energy as they consume, resulting in zero net energy consumption on an annual basis. These buildings typically achieve this through a combination of energy efficiency measures, on-site renewable energy generation, and energy storage systems. ZEBs are becoming increasingly popular as a way to reduce carbon emissions, combat climate change, and lower operating costs for building owners.</p><p>The Zero Energy Buildings (ZEBs) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.
