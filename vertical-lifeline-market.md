<p><p>Vertical lifelines are essential safety equipment used in various industries for fall protection. There are two main types of vertical lifelines in the market: rigid and flexible. Rigid vertical lifelines are made of durable materials like steel or aluminum and provide a fixed anchor point for workers to attach their fall protection equipment. On the other hand, flexible vertical lifelines are made of synthetic materials like rope or webbing, offering more mobility and flexibility for workers while still ensuring their safety at heights. Both types play a crucial role in preventing falls and protecting workers in elevated work environments.</p></p>
