<p><p>DDGS (Dried Distillers' Grains with Solubles) Feed is a co-product of ethanol production that has gained popularity as an animal feed ingredient due to its high nutritional value. It is produced by removing starch from corn and then fermenting the remaining components to produce ethanol. The byproduct, DDGS, is then dried to create a protein-rich feed supplement for livestock, poultry, and aquaculture.</p><p>The DDGS Feed Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to increasing consumer demand for protein-rich animal feed, as well as the sustainable nature of utilizing a byproduct from ethanol production.
