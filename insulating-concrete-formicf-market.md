<p><p>Insulating Concrete Form (ICF) is a construction method that uses interlocking modular units to create a mold for concrete walls. There are three main types of ICF systems: flat wall systems, grid wall systems, and post & lintel systems. Flat wall systems are the most common and feature a flat surface for easy finishing. Grid wall systems have a grid-like pattern for added strength. Post & lintel systems use vertical and horizontal components for structural support. Each system offers unique benefits for different construction needs.</p></p>
