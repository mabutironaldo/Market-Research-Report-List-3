<p><p>Gastrointestinal videoscopes are medical devices used to examine the gastrointestinal tract. There are two main types: hard mirror and flexible soft mirror videoscopes. Hard mirror videoscopes have a rigid structure with a mirror at the end for imaging, while flexible soft mirror videoscopes have a flexible shaft and a soft mirror at the tip. Both types allow for high-quality visualization of the gastrointestinal tract, with the flexible soft mirror videoscopes being more versatile and able to access difficult-to-reach areas.</p></p>
