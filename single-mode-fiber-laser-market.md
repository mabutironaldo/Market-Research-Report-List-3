<p><p>Single-mode fiber laser market is divided into low power fiber laser and medium power fiber laser segments. Low power fiber lasers have output power typically below 1 kW and are utilized for applications such as marking, engraving, and welding of small components. On the other hand, medium power fiber lasers have output power ranging from 1 kW to 10 kW and find applications in cutting, welding, and additive manufacturing processes. Both types of fiber lasers offer high beam quality, efficiency, and reliability, making them popular choices in industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
