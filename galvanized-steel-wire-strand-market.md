<p><ul><li>1X3</li><li>1X7</li><li>1X19</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized steel wire strands come in various types based on the number of wires twisted together. 1X3 strand consists of three wires twisted together, 1X7 consists of seven wires twisted together, and 1X19 consists of nineteen wires twisted together. Each type has its own specific strength and flexibility characteristics, making them suitable for different applications. 1X3 strands are commonly used in applications requiring high tensile strength, 1X7 strands are ideal for suspension bridges and structures that require flexibility, while 1X19 strands are commonly used in cable barriers and overhead power lines.</p></p>
