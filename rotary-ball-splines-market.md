<p><p>Rotary Ball Splines SPB and Rotary Ball Splines SPR are both types of rotary ball splines used in various industrial applications. The SPB market offers splines with balls arranged in a circular track, allowing for smooth linear motion and high precision. On the other hand, the SPR market provides splines with balls arranged in a helical track, offering greater load-carrying capacity and better resistance to misalignment. Both types of rotary ball splines are widely used in machinery and equipment requiring linear motion transmission.</p></p>
