<p><p>Diagnostic electrophysiology catheters are used to diagnose and treat heart rhythm disorders. Circular mapping catheters are designed to create a precise three-dimensional map of the heart's electrical activity, enabling accurate diagnosis and treatment. Grid mapping catheters use a grid-like array of electrodes to provide a detailed view of the heart's electrical signals, helping physicians locate and treat abnormalities. Both types of catheters play a crucial role in diagnostic electrophysiology procedures, providing valuable information and aiding in the effective management of heart rhythm disorders.</p></p>
