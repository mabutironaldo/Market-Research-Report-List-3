POM-H refers to the homopolymer grade of POM, which offers excellent mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. On the other hand, POM-C refers to the copolymer grade of POM, which provides better impact resistance and low-temperature toughness. Both POM-H and POM-C have diverse applications in automotive, electronics, consumer goods, and industrial sectors due to their superior performance and reliability.</p></p>
