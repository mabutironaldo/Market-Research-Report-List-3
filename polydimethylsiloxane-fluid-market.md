<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane Fluid Market has three main types based on viscosity: Low Viscosity, Medium Viscosity, and High Viscosity. Low Viscosity fluids are thin and flow easily, making them suitable for applications where fast spreading or penetration is required. Medium Viscosity fluids have a balance of flow and resistance, making them versatile for various uses. High Viscosity fluids are thick and sticky, ideal for applications requiring high stability or adhesion.
