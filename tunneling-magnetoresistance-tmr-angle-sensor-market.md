Digital output sensors provide a clear on/off signal based on the angle of rotation, making them easy to integrate into digital systems. On the other hand, Analog output sensors provide a continuous output signal that changes proportionally with the angle of rotation, offering more precise measurements for applications that require fine adjustments. Both types cater to different needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics.</p></p>
