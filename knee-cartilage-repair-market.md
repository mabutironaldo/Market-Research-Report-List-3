<p><p>The knee cartilage repair market is divided into various types of procedures. Arthroscopic chondroplasty involves removing damaged cartilage through minimally invasive surgery. Autologous chondrocyte procedures use a patient's own cells to regenerate cartilage. Osteochondral grafts transplantation involves transferring healthy cartilage and bone from a donor to the affected area. Cell-based cartilage resurfacing utilizes tissue engineering techniques to cultivate new cartilage cells. Implant transplant procedures involve replacing damaged cartilage with artificial implants. Microfracture stimulates the growth of new cartilage by creating tiny fractures in the bone. The market also includes other products for knee cartilage repair.</p></p>
