On the other hand, Li-Ion batteries are lighter, more energy-efficient, and have a longer lifespan, making them ideal for high-performance electric scooters and motorcycles. The market for Li-Ion batteries is growing rapidly due to advancements in technology and increasing demand for electric vehicles.</p></p>
