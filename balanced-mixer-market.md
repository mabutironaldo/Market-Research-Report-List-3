<p><p>Balanced mixers are used in RF and microwave circuits for frequency conversion. There are three main types of balanced mixers: Double Balanced Mixers, Triple Balanced Mixers, and Quadrature Mixers. Double Balanced Mixers provide good balance and rejection of LO and RF signals. Triple Balanced Mixers offer even better performance by adding a third balun to improve LO-RF isolation. Both types are widely used in applications requiring high linearity and spurious rejection, such as in radar systems and communication equipment.</p></p>
