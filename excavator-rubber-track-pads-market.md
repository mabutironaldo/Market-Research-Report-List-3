<p><ul><li>Bolt-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li><li>Clip-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li><li>Chain-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Excavator rubber track pads come in three main types: Bolt-On, Clip-On, and Chain-On. Bolt-On track pads are secured to the steel track shoes using bolts, providing a sturdy and permanent attachment. Clip-On track pads are easily installed and removed without the need for tools, offering convenience and flexibility. Chain-On track pads feature a chain design for quick installation and removal, making them ideal for temporary use or frequent track pad changes. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
