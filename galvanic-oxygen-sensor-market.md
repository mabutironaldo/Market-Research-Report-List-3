<p><p>Galvanic oxygen sensors are utilized in two primary markets: stress control and mass flow control. Stress control involves monitoring and regulating oxygen levels in environments with fluctuating oxygen levels, such as incubators or breathing apparatus. Mass flow control, on the other hand, focuses on measuring and controlling oxygen levels in applications where precise oxygen flow rates are critical, such as in industrial processes or medical equipment. Both markets rely on galvanic oxygen sensors to ensure accurate and reliable oxygen monitoring and control.</p></p>
