<p><p>Composite coatings market includes various types such as Laser Melt Injection, Brazing, Electroless Plating, and others. Laser Melt Injection involves injecting a hard material into a substrate using laser melting. Brazing is a process of joining materials using a filler metal that melts above 450°C. Electroless Plating is a method of depositing a metal coating on a substrate through a chemical reaction. Other types of composite coatings may include thermal spray coatings, chemical vapor deposition, and physical vapor deposition.</p></p>
