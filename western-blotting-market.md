<p><p>Western blotting is a widely used laboratory technique that allows researchers to identify and analyze specific proteins in a complex mixture. It involves the separation of proteins based on their size using gel electrophoresis, followed by their transfer to a solid membrane. The transferred proteins are then detected using specific antibodies that bind to the target protein. This technique is commonly used in molecular biology and biochemistry research to study protein expression and detect disease biomarkers.</p><p>The Western Blotting Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cancer and infectious diseases, is one of the key factors driving market growth. Western blotting is widely used in the diagnosis and monitoring of various diseases, which is contributing to its increased adoption.</p><p>Moreover, the advancements in technology and the development of novel products are further propelling market growth.
