<p><p>Hot-Pressed Magnets are widely used in various industries and come in two main types: Injection Magnets and Pressed Magnets. Injection Magnets are made by injecting a mixture of magnetic powder and binder into a mold, while Pressed Magnets are made by pressing a mixture of magnetic powder and binder together under high pressure and heat. Both types of hot-pressed magnets offer strong magnetic properties and are used in applications such as electric motors, sensors, and actuators.</p></p>
