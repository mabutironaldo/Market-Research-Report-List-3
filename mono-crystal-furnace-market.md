<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mono-Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mono-crystal furnace market includes two main types: Czochralski (CZ) method furnace and Floating Zone (FZ) method furnace. CZ method furnace is commonly used for producing mono-crystals with uniform structure and low defect density, making it ideal for semiconductor and solar industries. On the other hand, FZ method furnace is known for producing high-purity and defect-free mono-crystals, making it suitable for research and specialized applications in industries such as aerospace and healthcare.</p></p>
