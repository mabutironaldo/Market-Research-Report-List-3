<p><ul><li>Electric Oil Pump</li><li>Mechanical Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive oil pump market includes electric oil pumps and mechanical oil pumps. Electric oil pumps are powered by electricity and offer more precise control over oil flow, making them suitable for modern vehicles with advanced engine technology. On the other hand, mechanical oil pumps are driven by the engine itself through a belt or gear system, providing reliable performance but less flexibility in terms of oil flow control. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining proper lubrication for the engine and ensuring optimal performance.</p></p>
