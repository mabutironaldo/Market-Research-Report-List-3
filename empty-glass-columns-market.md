<p><strong>The Empty Glass Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Packed Columns</li><li>Capillary Columns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Empty glass columns are used in gas chromatography for separating and analyzing compounds. There are two main types of empty glass columns: packed columns and capillary columns. Packed columns contain a stationary phase material packed into the column, while capillary columns have a thin film of stationary phase coated on the inner wall of the column. Packed columns are ideal for separating larger molecules, while capillary columns are better suited for separating smaller molecules. Both types of columns are essential in various analytical applications.</p></p>
