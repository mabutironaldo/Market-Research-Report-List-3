<p><p>Industrial Automation Control market consists of various types of automation systems used in industries. Sensors are devices that measure and detect physical properties, providing feedback to control systems. Distributed Control Systems (DCS) are computerized systems that monitor and manage industrial processes. Drives are electronic devices that control the speed and torque of motors. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems are used for remote monitoring and control of industrial processes. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) are ruggedized computers used to automate industrial machinery and processes. These systems collectively enable efficient and automated control of industrial operations.</p></p>
