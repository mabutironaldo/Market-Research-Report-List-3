<p><strong>The Galvanized Iron (GI) Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Pipes</li><li>Welded Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized Iron (GI) Pipes market consists of two main types: Seamless Pipes and Welded Pipes. Seamless Pipes are produced without any welding seams, providing a smooth and continuous surface. They are commonly used in high-pressure applications. On the other hand, Welded Pipes are created by welding a strip of metal into a cylindrical shape. They are less expensive and are often used for lower pressure applications. Both types of pipes are coated with zinc to protect against corrosion, making them durable and long-lasting.</p></p>
