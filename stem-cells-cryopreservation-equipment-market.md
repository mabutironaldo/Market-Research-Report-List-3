<p><p>Stem cells cryopreservation equipment market consists of two main types: liquid phase and vapor phase. Liquid phase cryopreservation equipment allows for direct contact between the stem cells and liquid nitrogen, ensuring fast freezing rates and efficient preservation. On the other hand, vapor phase cryopreservation equipment uses the vapor form of nitrogen to cool down the stem cells gradually, reducing the risk of ice formation and cellular damage. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the successful preservation of stem cells for future use.</p></p>
