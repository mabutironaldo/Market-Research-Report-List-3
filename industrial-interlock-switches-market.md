<p><p>Industrial interlock switches are crucial safety devices in manufacturing plants and industrial settings. Guard locking switches are designed to prevent access to hazardous areas by keeping guards securely locked until the machine is in a safe state. Hinge switches use a hinged mechanism to detect when a guard door is opened, triggering a safety stop. Other types of interlock switches include tongue-operated switches, magnetic switches, and non-contact switches, each serving specific safety needs in industrial environments.</p></p>
