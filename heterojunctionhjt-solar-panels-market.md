<p><p>Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels are available in two main market types: monofacial cell and bifacial cell. Monofacial cell panels have one active side that absorbs sunlight, while bifacial cell panels have two active sides that can capture sunlight and convert it into electricity. Bifacial cell panels are able to generate more energy as they can utilize sunlight from both the front and back sides, making them more efficient than monofacial cell panels.</p></p>
