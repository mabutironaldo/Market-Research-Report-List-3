<p><p>Flexible command cable market types include single core and multi-core options. Single core cables consist of a single insulated conductor, making them ideal for applications that require a simple, straightforward connection. On the other hand, multi-core cables have multiple insulated conductors housed within a single cable sheath, offering greater flexibility and versatility for more complex wiring setups. Both single core and multi-core cables cater to different needs in the market, providing solutions for a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
