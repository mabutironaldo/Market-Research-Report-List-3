<p><p>Neuromodulation sensors are categorized into three types based on their operations: Physical Quantity Sensors measure parameters like temperature or pressure, Chemical Volume Sensors detect chemical concentrations in the body, and Biomass Sensors monitor biological signals. Each type plays a crucial role in neuromodulation therapies by providing real-time data on bodily functions. Physical Quantity Sensors help in maintaining appropriate stimulation levels, Chemical Volume Sensors ensure accurate drug delivery, and Biomass Sensors track the response of neuronal tissues to the treatment.</p></p>
