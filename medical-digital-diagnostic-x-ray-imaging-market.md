<p><p>The Medical Digital Diagnostic X-Ray Imaging market consists of two main types: Stationary X-rays and Portable X-rays. Stationary X-rays are traditional X-ray machines that are fixed in a specific location, typically in hospitals or clinics. They are larger and have higher imaging capabilities, allowing for detailed examinations. On the other hand, Portable X-rays are smaller and can be easily moved from one location to another. They are commonly used in emergency situations or for patients who are unable to move.</p></p>
