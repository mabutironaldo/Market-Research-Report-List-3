<p><p>X-Ray film processors are categorized into two main types: Manual X-Ray Film Processors and Automatic X-Ray Film Processors. Manual processors require the user to manually feed the film through the processor, while automatic processors automate the entire process, from film feeding to developing and drying. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the market, with manual processors offering more control and flexibility, while automatic processors provide convenience and efficiency in high-volume settings.</p></p>
