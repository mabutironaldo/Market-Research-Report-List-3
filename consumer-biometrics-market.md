<p><p>Consumer biometrics refers to the use of biometric technology in various consumer applications, such as smartphones, wearable devices, and smart home appliances, to enhance security, convenience, and personalization. Firstly, the increasing adoption of biometric technology in smartphones and wearable devices is a major driver for market growth.
