<p><p>The porcelain insulators market can be divided into breakdown type and non-breakdown type. Breakdown type insulators are designed to isolate high voltage electricity and prevent current leakage. They are commonly used in power transmission and distribution systems. Non-breakdown type insulators are used in low voltage applications to support and protect electrical conductors. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical systems, with breakdown type insulators being more critical in high voltage applications.</p></p>
