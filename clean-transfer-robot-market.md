<p><p>Clean Transfer Robot Market can be divided into two main types: Atmospheric Transfer Robots and Vacuum Transfer Robots. Atmospheric Transfer Robots operate in normal air conditions and are suitable for transferring objects in clean environments. Vacuum Transfer Robots, on the other hand, are designed to work in vacuum environments, ensuring the transportation of objects without being affected by external air pressure. Both types of robots play a crucial role in various industries such as semiconductors, electronics, and pharmaceuticals for clean and efficient material handling processes.</p></p>
