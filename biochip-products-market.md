<p><p>Biochip products are divided into two main categories: Active Chips and Passive Chips. Active Chips contain electronic components that enable them to perform functions such as processing signals and data. They are typically used in applications that require complex operations. On the other hand, Passive Chips do not contain electronic components and instead rely on external sources for power and data processing. They are often used in simpler applications where basic information storage or retrieval is sufficient.</p></p>
