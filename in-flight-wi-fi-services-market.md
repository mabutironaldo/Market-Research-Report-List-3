<p><p>In-Flight Wi-Fi services are typically provided through two main types of technology: air-to-ground and satellite. Air-to-ground technology requires ground-based towers to transmit signals to the aircraft, providing a stable and cost-effective option for domestic flights. On the other hand, satellite technology utilizes satellites orbiting the Earth to provide broader coverage and faster speeds, making it ideal for international flights or remote areas. Both technologies cater to different market segments within the In-Flight Wi-Fi services industry, offering passengers seamless connectivity during their travels.</p></p>
