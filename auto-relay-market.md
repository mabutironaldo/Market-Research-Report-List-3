<p><p>Auto relay market consists of two main types: Plug-In Relay and PCB Relay. Plug-In Relays are traditional relays that are easily replaceable and can be plugged into sockets without soldering. On the other hand, PCB Relays are directly mounted on printed circuit boards for compact size and high reliability. Both types of relays play a crucial role in controlling electrical circuits in automobiles, ensuring smooth operation of various systems such as lights, air conditioning, and engine control.</p></p>
