<p><p>Retinal vascular OCT equipment market is classified into different types based on technology. SD-OCT, or spectral-domain OCT, offers high resolution and fast imaging. Swept-source OCT provides deeper penetration and is ideal for imaging the choroid. Other types of OCT equipment may include time-domain OCT and enhanced depth imaging OCT. Each type offers unique capabilities and features for visualizing the retinal vasculature and assessing vascular disorders. Overall, these technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring retinal vascular diseases.</p></p>
