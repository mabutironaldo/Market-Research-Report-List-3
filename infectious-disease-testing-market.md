<p><p>The infectious disease testing market can be categorized into various types. Immunodiagnostics involves the detection of specific antibodies or antigens in a patient's blood sample. Clinical microbiology focuses on identifying and characterizing the microorganisms responsible for infectious diseases. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a technique used to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences. INAAT (Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology) is a molecular method for detecting and identifying infectious agents. DNA sequencing and Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) analyze the genetic material of pathogens. DNA microarrays allow simultaneous detection of multiple pathogens. Other market segments include various diagnostic techniques and tools used for infectious disease testing.</p></p>
