<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) Wire market includes two primary types based on the conductor material - Aluminum Conductor and Copper Conductor. Aluminum conductor wires are generally more cost-effective but have lower conductivity compared to copper conductor wires. On the other hand, copper conductor wires are known for their higher conductivity and better performance, but they tend to be more expensive. The choice between the two types depends on factors like budget, installation requirements, and desired efficiency of the PV system.</p></p>
