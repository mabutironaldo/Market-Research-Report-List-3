<p><strong>The Universal Lateral Couplings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clamp Universal Lateral Couplings</li><li>Screw Universal Lateral Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal lateral couplings are used to connect two shafts while allowing for lateral movement. There are two main types in the market: clamp universal lateral couplings and screw universal lateral couplings. </p><p>Clamp universal lateral couplings use clamps to securely hold the shafts in place, while allowing for some flexibility and angulation. Screw universal lateral couplings use screws to hold the shafts together and provide a rigid connection. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications to transmit power and accommodate misalignment between shafts.</p></p>
