<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic level sensors are commonly categorized into two main types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors are designed to detect when a certain level of liquid or solid material has been reached, triggering an on/off signal. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, provide real-time monitoring of the material level, allowing for precise measurement and control. Both types are widely used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment for accurate and reliable level detection.</p></p>
