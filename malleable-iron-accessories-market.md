<p><p>Malleable Iron Accessories Market includes three main types: White Heart Malleable Iron, Black Heart Malleable Iron, and Pearlitic Malleable Iron. White Heart Malleable Iron has a relatively low strength but offers good ductility and is suitable for applications requiring slight deformation. Black Heart Malleable Iron is stronger than White Heart Malleable Iron and is commonly used in heavy-duty industrial applications. Pearlitic Malleable Iron is a combination of ferrite and pearlite structures, offering high strength and toughness, making it ideal for demanding engineering applications.</p></p>
