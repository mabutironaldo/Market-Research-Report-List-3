<p><p>Raised floor systems are typically categorized into two main types: steel plate and calcium sulfate board. Steel plate raised floors utilize high-strength steel panels that are durable and provide excellent support for heavy equipment in data centers and other industrial settings. On the other hand, calcium sulfate board raised floors are made of a composite material that offers strong fire resistance and acoustic performance, making them ideal for commercial office spaces where safety and comfort are paramount. Both types of raised floors provide flexible and efficient solutions for managing cables and airflow in buildings.</p></p>
