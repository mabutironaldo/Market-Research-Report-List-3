<p><p>Anti-glare rear view mirrors are divided into two main types: exterior mirrors and interior mirrors. Exterior mirrors are mounted on the outer sides of the vehicle and are used by drivers to see vehicles approaching from behind or to change lanes. Interior mirrors are located inside the vehicle and provide a reflection of the back of the vehicle. Both types of mirrors are designed to reduce the glare from headlights of vehicles behind, ensuring better visibility for the driver.</p></p>
