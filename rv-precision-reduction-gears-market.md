<p><p>RV precision reduction gears are commonly categorized into two main types: gear reducers and worm reducers. Gear reducers utilize gear mechanisms to reduce the speed of a rotating input while increasing its torque output. On the other hand, worm reducers use a worm gear to achieve the same result. Both types of reduction gears are widely used in various industrial applications where precision and efficiency are paramount, such as in robotics, automation, and machinery.</p></p>
