<p><strong>The Automotive Over the Air (OTA) Updates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SOTA</li><li>FOTA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Over the Air (OTA) Updates involve two main types of markets: Software Over the Air (SOTA) and Firmware Over the Air (FOTA). SOTA deals with updating the software in vehicles, such as infotainment systems or navigation apps, to enhance functionality and security. On the other hand, FOTA focuses on updating the firmware of hardware components in vehicles, such as engine control units or electronic control units, to improve performance and reliability. Both types of OTA updates play a crucial role in keeping vehicles up-to-date with the latest technology and advancements.</p></p>
