<p><p>The automotive brake caliper market consists of two main types: Single Piston Caliper and Multi-Piston Caliper. Single Piston Calipers have only one piston that applies pressure to the brake pads, while Multi-Piston Calipers have multiple pistons for more even pressure distribution and improved braking performance. Single piston calipers are commonly used in passenger cars and light trucks, while Multi-Piston Calipers are often found in high-performance vehicles and racing cars for enhanced braking power and control.</p></p>
