<p><p>The 300mm wafer used electrostatic chuck market consists of two main types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. </p><p>Coulomb type utilizes the principle of electrical attraction between positive and negative charges to hold the wafer in place. </p><p>On the other hand, JR type uses a combination of Coulomb forces and the Van der Waals force to achieve better thermal and mechanical stability. </p><p>Both types are widely used in semiconductor manufacturing for processing 300mm wafers.</p></p>
