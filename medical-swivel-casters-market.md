Kingpin-less casters offer improved maneuverability and stability due to their design without a central kingpin. Hollow kingpin casters have a hollow central kingpin, reducing weight and providing smoother movement. Plate casters have a flat metal mounting plate for easy installation and can support heavy loads. All these casters are essential components in medical equipment to ensure smooth movement, stability, and safety in healthcare settings.</p></p>
