<p><p>TLD Dosimeters, or Thermoluminescent Dosimeters, are commonly used in the market for quality assurance due to their ability to measure ionizing radiation doses. OSL Dosimeters, or Optically Stimulated Luminescence Dosimeters, offer similar capabilities as TLD dosimeters but are often preferred for their higher sensitivity. Other types of dosimeters in the market include film badges, electronic personal dosimeters, and pocket ionization chambers. These dosimeters play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and quality of radiation therapy treatments in various industries.</p></p>
