<p><strong>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline 3D AOI</li><li>Offline 3D AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of PCBs during the manufacturing process. These systems help detect defects quickly and make immediate adjustments, reducing the risk of defective products reaching the market. Offline 3D AOI systems, on the other hand, are typically used for post-production inspection and analysis. They are valuable for in-depth examination of PCBs and identifying quality issues that may have been missed during the inline inspection process.</p></p>
