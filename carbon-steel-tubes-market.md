<p><p>Carbon steel tubes can be divided into two main types: welded tubes and seamless tubes. Welded tubes are made by welding together steel strips or plates, resulting in a cost-effective option for various industrial applications. On the other hand, seamless tubes are manufactured without any seams, creating a strong and consistent product that is suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Both types of tubes play a vital role in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
