<p><strong>The Plastic Mould Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carburizing type</li><li>Pre hardened</li><li>Age hardening type</li><li>Corrosion-resistant type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic mould steel is available in various types to suit different applications. Carburizing type steel is designed for high surface hardness and wear resistance. Pre-hardened steel is ready to use for moulds without needing further heat treatment. Age hardening type steel can be hardened through aging process, offering improved toughness. Corrosion-resistant type steel is formulated to withstand corrosive environments. Each type of steel caters to specific needs in the plastic moulding industry, providing durability and reliability for various molding applications.</p></p>
