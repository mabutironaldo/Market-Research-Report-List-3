<p><p>Trimellitic Anhydride (TMA) is produced through two main methods - the Maleic Anhydride Catalytic (MC) method and the Methyl Glutaric Anhydride Catalytic (MGC) method. The MC method involves the catalytic oxidation of ortho-xylene while the MGC method utilizes the catalytic oxidation of mesitylene. Both methods result in the production of TMA, a key ingredient in the manufacturing of various products like plasticizers, polyimides, and coatings. The market for TMA produced through these methods remains strong due to the high demand for these end products.</p></p>
