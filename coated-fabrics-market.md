<p><p>Coated fabrics market can be broadly categorized into three main types: polymer coated fabrics, rubber coated fabrics, and others. Polymer coated fabrics are those coated with synthetic polymers like PVC, PU, or PE for added durability and waterproofing properties. Rubber coated fabrics, on the other hand, are coated with natural or synthetic rubbers for enhanced flexibility and resistance to abrasion. The "others" category includes fabrics coated with materials such as wax, silicones, or fluoropolymers for specialized applications like fire resistance or chemical protection.</p></p>
