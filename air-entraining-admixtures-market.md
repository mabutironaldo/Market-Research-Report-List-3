<p><p>Air-Entraining Admixtures are used in concrete production to create tiny air bubbles in the concrete mixture, which improves workability and durability. There are two main types of Air-Entraining Admixtures based on the presence of chloride. Air-Entraining Admixtures without Chloride are preferred for projects where chloride ion corrosion is a concern, such as marine environments. On the other hand, Air-Entraining Admixtures with Chloride are used in projects where additional chloride ions are needed for improved workability and freeze-thaw resistance.</p></p>
