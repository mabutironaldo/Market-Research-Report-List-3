<p><p>Fire beam detectors are a type of advanced fire detection system used to quickly identify the presence of smoke or flames in large indoor spaces. Infrared beam detectors work by sensing changes in infrared radiation caused by smoke particles, while optical beam detectors use reflected light to detect smoke. Both types are popular choices for high-ceilinged areas such as warehouses, atriums, and shopping malls due to their ability to cover large areas effectively and their resistance to false alarms from dust or steam.</p></p>
