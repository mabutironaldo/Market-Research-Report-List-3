<p><p>Wiper motor market typically consists of two main types - front windshield wiper motor and rear window wiper motor. Front windshield wiper motors are designed to operate the wipers on the front windshield of a vehicle, ensuring visibility in various weather conditions. Rear window wiper motors, on the other hand, are specifically engineered to control the wipers on the rear window of a vehicle, providing drivers with clear views from both the front and back of their vehicle. Both front and rear wiper motor markets cater to different needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
