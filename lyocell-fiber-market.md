<p><strong>The Lyocell Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Lyocell Fiber</li><li>Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Regular Lyocell Fiber is a type of cellulose fiber made from dissolving wood pulp through a solvent spinning process, resulting in a soft and breathable fabric. On the other hand, Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber undergoes a chemical process to create a stronger and more durable fiber, making it suitable for applications requiring higher strength and resilience. Both types of Lyocell Fiber are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional synthetic fibers, as they are biodegradable and produced in a closed-loop system that minimizes environmental impact.</p></p>
