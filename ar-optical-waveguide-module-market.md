<p><strong>The AR Optical Waveguide Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Waveguides</li><li>Diffractive Waveguides</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Optical Waveguide Module market includes three main types: Reflective Waveguides, Diffractive Waveguides, and Others. Reflective Waveguides use mirrors to reflect light within the waveguide, enabling AR display. Diffractive Waveguides use diffraction gratings to direct and manipulate light, offering a compact and lightweight design. The category of Others encompasses alternative waveguide technologies that may utilize different principles such as holographic or holographic optical elements. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of performance, size, and cost in the AR market.</p></p>
