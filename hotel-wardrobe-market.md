<p><p>The hotel wardrobe market can be categorized into four main types: standard, modular, corner, and wall-mounted. Standard wardrobes are traditional freestanding units that come in various sizes and designs. Modular wardrobes consist of interchangeable units that can be customized to fit different spaces. Corner wardrobes are designed to optimize space in the corners of a room. Wall-mounted wardrobes are attached directly to the wall, saving floor space and creating a sleek, modern look. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different design preferences and spatial requirements.</p></p>
