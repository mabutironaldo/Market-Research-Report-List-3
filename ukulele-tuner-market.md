<p><p>The Ukulele Tuner market is categorized into four major types based on size and tuning range - Soprano, Concert, Tenor, and Baritone. Soprano ukuleles are the smallest and typically have a higher pitch, while Concert ukuleles are slightly larger and produce a richer tone. Tenor ukuleles have a longer neck and deeper sound, suitable for a wider range of music styles. Baritone ukuleles are the largest, with a lower tuning similar to a guitar, making them popular among musicians looking for a bass-like sound in a compact instrument.</p></p>
