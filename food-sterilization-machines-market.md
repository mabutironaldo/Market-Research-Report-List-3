<p><p>Food sterilization machines use various methods to eliminate harmful microorganisms and extend the shelf life of food products. The types of sterilization methods include heat, steam, radiation, chemical, filtration, and others. Heat sterilization involves applying high temperatures to the food product. Steam sterilization uses steam to kill pathogens. Radiation sterilization uses high-energy rays to eliminate microorganisms. Chemical sterilization involves using chemicals like hydrogen peroxide. Filtration sterilization removes microorganisms through physical filtration methods. Other methods may include pulsed light, ultrasound, and ozone.</p></p>
