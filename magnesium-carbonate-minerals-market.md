<p><p>Magnesium Carbonate Minerals can be divided into two main types: Magnesite and Hydromagnesite. Magnesite is a natural mineral composed of magnesium carbonate, commonly found in deposits around the world. It is widely used in various industries, such as construction, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals. Hydromagnesite, on the other hand, is a hydrated magnesium carbonate mineral that forms under specific conditions. It is often used in the production of flame-retardant materials and as a filler in plastics and rubber products.</p></p>
