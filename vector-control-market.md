<p><p>Vector control markets can be categorized into several types based on the methods used to eliminate or manage vectors. Chemical control involves the use of pesticides and insecticides to kill or repel vectors. Physical and mechanical control methods include trapping, barriers, and environmental modifications. Biological control uses natural enemies of vectors to reduce their populations. Other types of vector control may include genetic control methods or innovative technologies.
