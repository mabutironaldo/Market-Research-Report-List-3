<p><p>Handheld type image barcode readers are portable devices that are held by hand and moved towards the barcode to scan it. They are commonly used in retail stores for inventory management and checkout processes. On the other hand, stationary type image barcode readers are fixed in one position and can scan barcodes automatically as they pass by the reader. They are often used in logistics and manufacturing facilities for fast and accurate scanning of items. Both types play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and accuracy of barcode scanning processes.</p></p>
