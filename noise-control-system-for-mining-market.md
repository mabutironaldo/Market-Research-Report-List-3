<p><ul><li>Internal Noise Control System</li><li>External Noise Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal Noise Control System for mining involves implementing measures within the mining equipment or machinery to reduce noise emissions. This can include using soundproofing materials, vibration isolation techniques, and modifying the design of the equipment. External Noise Control System, on the other hand, focuses on reducing noise at the source through barriers, enclosures, and landscaping to minimize the impact of noise on the surrounding environment and communities. Both systems aim to create a safer and more sustainable working environment for miners while minimizing noise pollution.</p></p>
