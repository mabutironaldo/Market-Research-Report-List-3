<p><p>Walk-behind trowels come in two main types: hydrostatic and mechanical. Hydrostatic walk-behind trowels are powered by hydraulic systems, providing smooth and precise control over the machine's movements. These trowels are preferred for larger projects due to their high efficiency. On the other hand, mechanical walk-behind trowels are driven by traditional mechanical systems, offering reliable performance for smaller projects. Both types are widely used in construction for finishing concrete surfaces with precision and efficiency.</p></p>
