<p><p>Central Tire Inflation System (CTIS) technology is available in two main types: Manual CTIS and Automatic CTIS. Manual CTIS allows the driver to adjust tire pressure manually based on the driving conditions, such as off-road terrain or highway driving. Automatic CTIS, on the other hand, uses sensors to continuously monitor tire pressure and automatically adjust it accordingly. Both systems offer benefits in terms of improved traction, fuel efficiency, and overall vehicle performance in various driving conditions.</p></p>
