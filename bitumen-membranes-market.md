<p><p>Bitumen membranes are widely used in the construction industry for waterproofing applications. There are three main types in the market: APP (Atactic Polypropylene), SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), and SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene). APP membranes are known for their resistance to UV rays and high temperatures. SBS membranes offer flexibility and durability, making them ideal for areas with temperature fluctuations. SEBS membranes are a newer option that provide excellent elasticity and resistance to environmental factors. Each type has its own unique properties to suit different project requirements.</p></p>
