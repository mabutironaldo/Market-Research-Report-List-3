<p><p>Flexible hysteroscopes are medical devices used for visualizing the inside of the uterus. There are two main types of flexible hysteroscopes: flexible video hysteroscopes and flexible fiberoptic hysteroscopes. </p><p>Flexible video hysteroscopes use advanced imaging technology to provide high-quality images and real-time video feed during the procedure. They offer better visualization and clarity, allowing for accurate diagnosis and treatment. </p><p>On the other hand, flexible fiberoptic hysteroscopes use a bundle of optical fibers to transmit light, providing illumination for visual examination. They are more cost-effective than video hysteroscopes but may not provide the same level of image quality.</p></p>
