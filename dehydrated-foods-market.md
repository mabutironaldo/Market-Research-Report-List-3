<p><p>Dehydrated foods market can be categorized into different types based on the dehydration process. Spray-dried foods involve spraying liquid food onto hot air to remove moisture. Freeze-dried foods are frozen first and then dried under vacuum to preserve nutrients. Vacuum-dried foods are dried under low pressure to retain flavor and texture. Other types of dehydrated foods may include sun-dried, air-dried, or drum-dried products. Each method offers unique benefits in terms of taste, texture, and nutritional content.</p></p>
