<p><p>Strip parquet floors are a popular choice for homeowners looking for a classic and elegant flooring option. In the market, there are two main types of strip parquet floors - engineered and solid. Engineered strip parquet floors are made up of multiple layers of wood veneers with a hardwood top layer, offering greater stability and resistance to moisture. Solid strip parquet floors, on the other hand, are made from a single piece of hardwood throughout, providing a traditional and timeless look. Both engineered and solid strip parquet floors cater to different preferences and needs of customers in the flooring market.</p></p>
