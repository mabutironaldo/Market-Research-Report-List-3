<p><p>Runway lighting refers to the lighting systems installed in runways to aid the safe takeoff and landing of aircraft. These lighting systems provide visual guidance to pilots, especially during low visibility conditions such as bad weather or nighttime operations. The lighting includes threshold lights, runway edge lights, centerline lights, touchdown zone lights, and taxiway lights, among others.</p><p>The runway lighting market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The growth can be attributed to several factors, including the expansion of airports, the rise in air passenger traffic, and increasing investments in airport infrastructure development. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on improving runway safety and navigation systems is driving the demand for advanced lighting solutions.</p><p>Additionally, the runway lighting market is also witnessing technological advancements and the integration of smart lighting solutions. These advancements include the use of LED lights, which provide energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and better brightness compared to traditional lighting systems.
