<p><ul><li>Cooling</li><li>Lubrication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cutting fluid market includes two main types: cooling and lubrication. Cooling fluids are designed to dissipate heat generated during the machining process, preventing damage to the cutting tool and workpiece. Lubrication fluids, on the other hand, reduce friction between the tool and workpiece, prolonging tool life and improving surface finish. Both types of fluids play a crucial role in enhancing machining efficiency and productivity by maintaining optimal operating conditions and reducing wear and tear on cutting tools.</p></p>
