<p><p>Softgel encapsulation equipment market can be categorized into two types: manual encapsulation equipment and automatic encapsulation equipment. Manual encapsulation equipment requires operators to fill gelatin shells with liquid formulations manually, limiting production capacity. On the other hand, automatic encapsulation equipment automates the process by removing the need for manual labor, increasing production efficiency and capacity. Automatic equipment is preferred by manufacturers looking to scale up production and improve overall productivity in softgel manufacturing.</p></p>
