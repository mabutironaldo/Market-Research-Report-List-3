<p><p>Membrane separation systems are widely used for various applications such as purification, filtration, and separation. These systems include microfiltration, ultrafiltration, nanofiltration, reverse osmosis, chromatography, and ion exchange. Microfiltration is used for large particle removal, ultrafiltration for medium-sized particles, nanofiltration for small particles, and reverse osmosis for removing dissolved solids. Chromatography is used for separating compounds based on their size and charge, while ion exchange removes ions from a solution. Each of these types offers unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
