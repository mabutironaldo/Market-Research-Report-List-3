<p><ul><li>Endothermic Film</li><li>Reflective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat insulating films are used to reduce heat transfer through windows and glass surfaces, providing energy efficiency and improving indoor comfort. Two common types of heat insulating films are endothermic films and reflective films. </p><p>Endothermic films absorb heat and release it slowly, helping to regulate indoor temperatures. Reflective films, on the other hand, reflect heat and light away from the building, reducing solar heat gain. Both types of films are effective in reducing energy costs and improving overall building performance.</p></p>
