<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft market consists of three main types: Vectored Thrust, Multirotor, and Lift + Cruise. Vectored Thrust eVTOLs use rotors that can change their direction to provide both vertical and horizontal flight. Multirotor eVTOLs have multiple fixed rotors that enable vertical take-off and landing. Lift + Cruise eVTOLs combine fixed wings for efficient cruising with electric propellers or rotors for vertical take-off and landing capabilities. These different types cater to various requirements and applications within the evolving eVTOL aircraft market.</p></p>
