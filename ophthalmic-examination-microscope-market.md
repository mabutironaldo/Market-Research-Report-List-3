<p><p>Ophthalmic Examination Microscopes are classified into two main types based on their mobility: On Casters and Fixed. On Casters microscopes are equipped with wheels for easy movement within the healthcare facility, providing flexibility in exam room setups. Fixed microscopes are stationary and typically installed in a specific location, offering stable and consistent positioning during examinations. Both types offer high-quality magnification and lighting for detailed visualization of the eye, assisting healthcare professionals in diagnosing and treating ocular conditions.</p></p>
