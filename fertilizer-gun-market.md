<p><strong>The Fertilizer Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sprayer</li><li>Deep Root</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fertilizer gun market consists of two main types: Sprayer and Deep Root. Sprayer fertilizer guns are designed for surface application, covering a larger area with their spray nozzles. They are typically used for lawns, gardens, and small agricultural plots. On the other hand, Deep Root fertilizer guns are specialized for injecting nutrients directly into the soil, reaching the roots of plants for more effective fertilization. They are commonly used in larger agricultural settings or for trees and shrubs that require deep feeding.</p></p>
