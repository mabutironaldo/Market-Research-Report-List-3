<p><p>PC VR Headsets market can be categorized into three types based on motion tracking capabilities. The 3DOF motion tracking type allows for basic movement tracking, such as looking around in a virtual environment. The 6DOF motion tracking type offers more advanced tracking capabilities including tracking positional movement. The 9DOF motion tracking type provides the highest level of tracking precision by incorporating additional sensors for more accurate tracking of movement and orientation in the virtual world. These different types cater to varying levels of immersive VR experiences.</p></p>
