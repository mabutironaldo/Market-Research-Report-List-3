<p><p>Industrial media converters are used to convert signals from one type of media to another, such as copper to fiber optic. Standalone media converters are individual units that are typically compact in size and easy to install, making them ideal for small-scale deployments. Chassis-based media converters, on the other hand, are larger units that can accommodate multiple media converter modules within a single chassis, allowing for greater scalability and flexibility in larger network environments. Both types offer reliable signal conversion and connectivity solutions for industrial applications.</p></p>
