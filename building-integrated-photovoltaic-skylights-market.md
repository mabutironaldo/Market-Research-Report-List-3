<p><p>Building Integrated Photovoltaic Skylights are solar panels that are integrated into the design of a building's skylight. Two main types are Crystalline Panel and Thin Film Panel. Crystalline panels are made of silicon and are more efficient, durable, and cost-effective. Thin film panels are lighter, flexible, and better suited for curved surfaces. Both types can generate electricity from sunlight and are a sustainable way to power buildings while maximizing natural light intake. The market for these technologies is growing as more buildings seek to incorporate renewable energy sources.</p></p>
