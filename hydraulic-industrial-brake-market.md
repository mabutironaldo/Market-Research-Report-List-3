<p><p>The hydraulic industrial brake market is categorized into three main types: disc brakes, drum brakes, and others. Disc brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a disc rotor to create friction and stop the motion. Drum brakes use brake shoes that press against the inner surface of a spinning drum to slow down or stop the vehicle. Other types of brakes in the market include emergency brakes, parking brakes, and electromagnetic brakes. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the industrial sector.</p></p>
