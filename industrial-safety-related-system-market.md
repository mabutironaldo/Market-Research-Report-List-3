<p><ul><li>Process Safety System</li><li>Emergency Interlock Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial safety related system market includes two main types: process safety systems and emergency interlock systems. Process safety systems are designed to prevent accidents and incidents by continuously monitoring and controlling various parameters in industrial processes. They effectively identify potential hazards, trigger alarms, and automatically shut down systems to prevent potential accidents. On the other hand, emergency interlock systems provide an additional layer of protection by automatically shutting down equipment or processes during critical situations or emergencies to avoid hazards and ensure the safety of personnel and assets.</p></p>
