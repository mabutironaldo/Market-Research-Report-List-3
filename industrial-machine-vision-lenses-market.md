<p><p>Industrial machine vision lenses are essential components in machine vision systems, providing image clarity and accuracy for various industrial applications. There are different market types based on the mounting options available. C-Mount lenses are widely used and compatible with most machine vision cameras. CS-Mount lenses have a smaller back focal length, making them ideal for compact camera setups. F-Mount lenses are commonly used in high-resolution applications. S-Mount lenses are smaller and typically used in consumer electronics. Other market types include specialized mounts tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
