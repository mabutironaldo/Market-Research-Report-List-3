<p><p>Polyethylene foamed plastics are manufactured using three main methods - mechanical, physical, and chemical. </p><p>1. Mechanical method involves physically agitating the polyethylene to create foam by incorporating air or gas bubbles in the material.</p><p> </p><p>2. Physical method entails using pressure and temperature changes to induce foaming in the polyethylene.</p><p>3. Chemical method involves introducing chemical agents that react with the polyethylene to generate foam.</p><p>Each method results in the creation of lightweight, insulating, and versatile foamed plastics used in various industries.</p></p>
