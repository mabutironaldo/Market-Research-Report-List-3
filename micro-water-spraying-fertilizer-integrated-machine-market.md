<p><p>Film Irrigation and Under Film Irrigation are two types of micro water spraying fertilizer integrated machines used in agriculture. Film Irrigation involves laying a plastic film over the soil surface before planting. This film helps to conserve water, control weeds, and promote healthy plant growth. Under Film Irrigation involves placing the water and fertilizer delivery system underneath the plastic film, ensuring more efficient and targeted application of nutrients to the plants' root zones. Both methods are popular choices for modern agricultural practices.</p></p>
