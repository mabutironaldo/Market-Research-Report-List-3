<p><p>The bio-based fragrance market consists of different types of aromatic compounds derived from biological sources. Tetracyclic biological base aromatics are a type of fragrance compound consisting of four interconnected rings, which impart characteristic scents found in nature. Hexyclic biological base aromatics, on the other hand, are aromatic compounds with six interconnected rings, providing distinct fragrances sourced from natural materials. These types of bio-based fragrance compounds are utilized in various industries such as personal care, cosmetics, and household products to enhance scent profiles using sustainable ingredients.</p></p>
