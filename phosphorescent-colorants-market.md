<p><p>Phosphorescent colorants are categorized into two main types: organic phosphorescent colorants and inorganic phosphorescent colorants. Organic phosphorescent colorants are derived from carbon-based compounds and are known for their vibrant and bright hues. On the other hand, inorganic phosphorescent colorants are made from non-carbon-based materials such as metals and minerals, offering a wide range of colors and longer-lasting phosphorescent effects. Both types of phosphorescent colorants are used in various industries such as paints, coatings, plastics, and textiles.</p></p>
