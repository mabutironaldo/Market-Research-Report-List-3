<p><p>Retaining compounds are categorized based on their strength levels, including Medium Strength, High Strength, and Low Strength. Medium Strength retaining compounds are designed for applications where a balance between strength and disassembly is required. High Strength retaining compounds provide maximum strength for demanding applications where permanent bonding is needed. Low Strength retaining compounds are ideal for applications where disassembly is needed, offering a lower strength level for easier removal. Each type of retaining compound caters to different needs and requirements in various industries and applications.</p></p>
